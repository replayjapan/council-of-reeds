# Source Of Truth

## User's Original Words

> I want to know if we should build a very lightweight meeting-notes tool for small internal teams. It should not become a full project management system. I want a practical recommendation, not a huge roadmap.

## What I Want

Decide whether the product idea is worth prototyping.

## Audience

Small internal teams that need searchable meeting notes and follow-up tasks.

## Constraints

- Keep the product lightweight.
- Avoid a full project management feature set.
- Assume one developer can build the first prototype.
- Focus on practical differentiation.

## What To Avoid

- Complex workflows.
- Enterprise permissions.
- Calendar automation in version one.
- Large roadmap thinking.

## Final Deliverable

A short recommendation and first prototype scope.
