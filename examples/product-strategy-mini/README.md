# Product Strategy Mini Council

This is a small, sanitized example of a completed Council.

It shows:

- `context/source-of-truth.md`
- `prompt.md`
- Round 1 Reed responses
- `round-1/plai-notes.md`
- final positions
- `synthesis.md`

The topic is fictional.

## Scenario

A small software team wants to decide whether to build a lightweight meeting-notes tool for internal teams.

The Council's job is to recommend whether to build it, what to keep small, and what to avoid.
