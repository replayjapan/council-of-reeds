# Round 1

## Recommendation

Build a prototype only as a learning test. The product should prove whether users want structured meeting memory, not another notes app.

## First Version

- one note page per meeting
- decisions section
- action items section
- search
- copy/share summary

## Exclude

- AI summary in the first build unless notes quality is already strong
- calendar integration
- team administration
- project management views

## Biggest Risk

Distribution. Small teams already have Google Docs, Notion, Slack, and existing note tools.

## Strongest Counter

If the prototype does not include a unique workflow, users may try it once and return to their current tools.
