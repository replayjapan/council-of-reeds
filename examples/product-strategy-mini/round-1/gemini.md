# Round 1

## Recommendation

Prototype a narrow version. The first test should focus on recurring meetings where teams lose decisions and follow-ups.

## First Version

- recurring meeting workspace
- notes
- decisions
- follow-ups
- search by person or decision

## Exclude

- broad task management
- integrations
- permissions beyond a simple team
- templates beyond one default format

## Biggest Risk

The tool may be useful but not important enough for teams to adopt.

## Strongest Counter

The team should first interview users and inspect existing meeting notes before writing code.
